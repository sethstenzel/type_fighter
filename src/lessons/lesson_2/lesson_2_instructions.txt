Ok rookie... lesson two starts now.

This time, the drones are testing your middle fingers and your thumb control.
In this lesson we are adding 'D', 'K', and Spacebar.

When you see D, press the D key with your left middle finger.
When you see K, press the K key with your right middle finger.
When you see ␣, press the ␣ with either thumb.

Do not move your whole hand.
Do not look down at the keyboard.

Keep your fingers on the home row... Stay focused, pilot.

Here they come!
