Welcome back, pilot ... punctuation starts now.

Your new training keys are 'apostrophe' and 'hyphen'.

Press apostrophe and hyphen with your right pinky finger. These punctuation keys sit just past the home row.

You are not starting over. Every key from the earlier missions can still show up.

Before launch, settle your hands on the home row. Find F and J by touch, rehearse the new reach, then bring your eyes forward.

Small movements. Clean returns. No looking down if you can help it.

To launch the training simulation, press Spacebar when you are ready.
