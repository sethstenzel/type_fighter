Welcome back, pilot ... 

Your new training keys are 'R' and 'U'.

These keys use the left pointer finger and right pointer finger respectively. 

Like 'G' and 'H' these are stretch keys as will be every key after this.
To press 'R' and 'U' stretch up with your pointer fingers and press them.

Remember not to chase the keyboard with your hands, keep your fingers going back to your lauch position...
Do not look down if you can help it.
Small movements, steady hands, eyes forward.

To launch the training simulation, use either thumb to press the Spacebar key.
