Welcome back, pilot ... looks like you are getting the hang of this.

Your new training keys are 'S' and 'L'.

These keys use your left ring finger and right ringer.

You are not starting over. Every key from the earlier lessons will still be needed.
Your job is to add these two new keys while keeping your hands anchored and your eyes forward.

Before launch, place both hands on the home row.
Find 'F' and 'J' by touch if you need to reset your position.
Then prepare the fingers for 'S' and 'L'.

Do not look down if you can help it.
Small movements, steady hands, eyes forward.

In this mission, drones will use all keys you have learned so far, with extra focus on 'S' and 'L'.

To launch the training simulation, use either thumb to press the Spacebar.
