Ok rookie... lesson 3 starts now.

Your training pod will be at the center of the screen.
Enemy drones will appear and move toward your pod.

This mission adds S and L.
Main fingers: Left ring, right ring.

Drones can use the keys from this lesson and the keys you have already learned.
Press the matching key to fire at the drone.

Some drones take more than one shot to destroy.

Keep your hands anchored, press with the correct finger, and return to ready position after every shot.

New threat: mini bosses can appear in this mission. Mini bosses are purple pentagons. They take more shots to destroy, rotate as they move, and launch small yellow drones at your pod.

The mission will stop spawning new drones after 40 have appeared, but you do not win until the drone target is complete and every mini boss has been destroyed.

Here they come!
