Ok rookie... lesson 5 starts now.

Your training pod will be at the center of the screen.
Enemy drones will appear and move toward your pod.

This mission adds G and H.
Main fingers: Left index, right index.

Drones can use the keys from this lesson and the keys you have already learned.
Press the matching key to fire at the drone.

Some drones take more than one shot to destroy.

Keep your hands anchored, press with the correct finger, and return to ready position after every shot.

Final boss training begins in this mission.

After the drone spawn target is reached and all mini bosses are destroyed, a rotating hexagon boss will appear.

The hexagon has a light blue shield with three shield levels. Normal shots will not break the shield.

Starting now, ␣ also controls your new mega weapon. Hold ␣ while the mega meter has charge, then press a target key to fire a mega shot at that target.

The mega meter fills in five blocks. More blocks mean more damage. After a mega shot fires, the weapon waits briefly before it begins charging again.

A shield hit only counts if the mega shot has at least three blocks of charge. Each valid shield hit weakens the shield by one level. When the shield reaches zero, it stays down.

The boss body can only be destroyed while the shield is down, and it requires a full five-block mega shot.

The final boss fires a small yellow drone after it moves into orbit. Destroy it before it reaches your pod.

Here they come!
