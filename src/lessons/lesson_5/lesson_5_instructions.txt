Congratulations on your promotion Private. But don't let it go to your head.

You'll notice a new weapon upgrade "Mega Shot" at the top of your screen.

It is a powerful weapon, but does have a brief cool down after you use it.
The more charged it is when you hold spare bar and press a fire key, the more damage it will do.

Hitting greater drones like reds and oranges with a charge of at least 3 will destroy them completely, and they won't even spawn lesser drones on destruction.

You'll use your left pointer and right pointer to press the 'G' and 'H' key.

Take care Private. There is and end of mission elite drone, and if you're not careful it will wipe the floor with you.

The hexa-drone has a light blue shield with three shield charges you will need to drain.
Normal shots do nothing to the hexa-drone, not to it, or it's shields. Your pod won't even let you waste the energy trying.

Only a fully charged "Mega Shot" will be able to take down its shields, and will also be required to destroy it.

Watch out though, until you do destroy it, it will generate an army of lesser drones to try and take you out.

Welcome to the big leagues Private. Stay calm and stay focused.
