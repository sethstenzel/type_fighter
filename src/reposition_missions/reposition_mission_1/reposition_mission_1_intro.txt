REPOSITION MISSION 1

Command has flagged a fault line opening on the far side of the sector. Your
pod is on the wrong side of it, and the survey window is closing fast.

This is not a firefight. It is a race against your own momentum.

Your pod carries a limited bank of thrust. Every burn spends it, and it only
starts refilling once you let the engines rest. Lean on the throttle and you
will scream across the sector -- but with nothing left to slow you down, you
will sail straight past the mark and have to claw your way back.

Fly clean. Spend your thrust with intent. Settle onto the coordinates and hold
station long enough for the survey to lock.

Reposition to the marked coordinates and hold. Good luck out there, pilot.
