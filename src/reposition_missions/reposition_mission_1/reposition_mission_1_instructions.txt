OBJECTIVE

Reposition your pod to the green target zone, then hold your pod inside it for
10 seconds without leaving. The target starts far off screen to the left --
follow the blue arrow on your pod's orbit; it always points the way.

MOVING THE POD (hold Shift)

You fly like a real ship: each key fires the thruster on that side, and the pod
is pushed the opposite way.

  Shift + F  -  fire the RIGHT thruster  ->  pod moves LEFT (toward the target)
  Shift + S  -  fire the LEFT thruster   ->  pod moves RIGHT
  Shift + D  -  fire the BOTTOM thruster ->  pod moves UP
  Shift + E  -  fire the TOP thruster    ->  pod moves DOWN

Your pod keeps its momentum -- thrust only changes your speed, it never bleeds
off on its own. The longer you hold a thrust, the faster you go, up to the pod's
top speed.

THRUST IS LIMITED (the orange gauge)

Every second of thrust burns one charge from your thrust bank. Reaching top
speed drains it completely. When you stop thrusting, the bank waits 1 second,
then refills one charge per second. Spend it wisely: if you burn everything
racing in, you will have nothing left to brake with and will overshoot the
target -- then you must drift back and try again.

STAYING ALIVE

Type the letter on a drone to fire your turret at it. Your shields, defense
drones, mega shot, and time stop all work here if you have them unlocked.

Reach the target and hold for 10 seconds. You have 3 minutes.
