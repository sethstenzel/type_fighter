# Type Fighter Achievements

Achievements are earned by completing specific progress, performance, upgrade, or resource goals. Achievement rewards are granted once per player.

## Rank Achievements

| Achievement | Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Private | Reach Private rank. | 500 credits |
| Lieutenant | Reach Lieutenant rank. | 750 credits |
| Captain | Reach Captain rank. | 1,000 credits |
| Major | Reach Major rank. | 2,000 credits and third defense drone support |

## Perfect Mission Achievements

A perfect lesson means the player completed the lesson with no damage taken and 100% accuracy.

| Achievement | Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Seeking Perfection | Complete 5 lessons perfectly. | 300 credits |
| Nearing Perfection | Complete 20 lessons perfectly. | 300 credits |
| Total Perfection | Complete all 36 main lessons perfectly. | 300 credits |

## Upgrade And Resource Achievements

| Achievement | Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Living Forever | Reach the maximum of 99 lives. | 300 credits |
| Fully Upgraded | Purchase every upgrade in the upgrades modal at least once. | Unlocks the 6th shield slot |
| Quartermaster | Sell 50 lives and 50 shield charges. | 300 credits |

## Completion Achievement

| Achievement | Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Typing Master | Complete all main lessons. | 10,000 credits |

## Shield Achievement

| Achievement | Requirement | Reward |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Shields Up | Start a mission with 6 shield charges, use all 6 shield charges, and finish without taking damage. | 300 credits |
